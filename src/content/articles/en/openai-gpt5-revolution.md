## GPT-5 is here: a revolution?

OpenAI has unveiled GPT-5, its most advanced language model to date. With performance that far surpasses GPT-4 on all benchmarks, this new model marks a turning point for the industry.

### Key numbers

- **Reasoning**: +40% accuracy on complex logic tasks
- **Code**: solves 85% of competitive programming problems (vs 67% for GPT-4)
- **Multimodal**: natively processes text, image, audio, and video
- **Context**: 1 million token window

### Pricing: a barrier?

The GPT-5 API comes at a premium:
- Input: $30/million tokens
- Output: $60/million tokens

That's roughly 3x more expensive than GPT-4 Turbo, raising questions about economic viability for many startups.

### Impact on competition

Google (Gemini), Anthropic (Claude), and Meta (Llama) will need to accelerate. Competition in AI has never been fiercer.

### Our analysis

GPT-5 is technically impressive, but the real question remains: who's going to pay? Companies are increasingly focused on price-to-performance ratios, and open-source models are closing the gap fast.

**The number to remember**: OpenAI targets $10 billion in revenue for 2025.
