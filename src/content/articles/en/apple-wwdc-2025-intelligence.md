## Apple goes all-in on AI

At WWDC 2025, Apple presented an ambitious vision of artificial intelligence natively integrated across its entire ecosystem.

### Siri 2.0: finally smart?

The new Siri assistant features a proprietary language model capable of understanding conversation context and executing complex tasks across multiple applications.

> "We don't want AI to be a gimmick. We want it to be invisible and useful." — Tim Cook

### iOS 19: key features

- **Smart notification summaries**: AI groups and prioritizes your notifications
- **Photos AI**: advanced automatic editing with object removal
- **Smart Mail**: contextual suggested replies
- **Safari AI**: one-click web page summaries

### Impact on developers

Apple opens its AI API to third-party developers, creating a new ecosystem of intelligent applications. Developers can now integrate Apple's models directly into their apps.

### What it means for investors

Apple stock (AAPL) gained 3% in after-hours trading following these announcements. Analysts estimate that AI integration could drive a massive device upgrade cycle.

## Our verdict

Apple arrives late to generative AI but offers a unique approach: privacy first. Local data processing and deep OS integration could convince users who are reluctant to use cloud-based solutions.

**Stay tuned**: iOS 19 public beta expected in July 2025.
