## The historic milestone is reached

Bitcoin has officially surpassed $100,000, a major psychological level that many thought impossible just two years ago.

### Catalysts behind the rally

1. **Spot Bitcoin ETFs**: inflows continue to break records
2. **2024 Halving**: supply reduction taking effect
3. **Institutional adoption**: BlackRock, Fidelity, and Goldman Sachs accumulating
4. **Macro context**: Fed rate cut expectations

### Technical analysis

Bitcoin broke through resistance at $95,000 with record volume. Key levels to watch:
- Support: $92,000 then $85,000
- Resistance: $110,000 then $125,000

### Should you buy now?

**Bull case:**
- Post-halving bull cycles historically last 12-18 months
- Institutional adoption is just beginning
- Supply structurally limited to 21 million BTC

**Bear case:**
- RSI in overbought territory
- Risk of massive profit-taking
- Regulatory uncertainty remains

### Our advice

Never invest more than you can afford to lose. DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) remains the wisest strategy for retail investors.
